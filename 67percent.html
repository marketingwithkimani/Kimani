Work With Me
You’re Not Struggling With Marketing.
You’re struggling with timing.
And more dangerously…
You’ve been taught timing by people who don’t even operate in your market.
Because Most Of What You’ve Learned…
Wasn’t Built For You.
Most marketing playbooks you follow personally… were built in different realities. Different economies. Different buying behaviors. Different speeds of trust.
But here… things move differently.

Here, someone can think about buying land for 6 months.

Talk to friends. Ask family. Drive past the same plot three times. Save slowly. Doubt. Come back again.

And only then… decide.

But the marketing you’ve been using?
It behaves like the decision happens in one moment.
“Buy now.” “Limited offer.” “Last chance.”
That’s not how your market moves. And deep down… you already know that.
Let Me Show You Something You’ve Already Lived.
Not as a business owner. As yourself.
Think about the last time you wanted something serious. Not small. Something that required sacrifice.
A TV.
You didn’t just wake up and buy it. It started casually. Maybe you were at a friend’s place. You saw a bigger screen. Clearer picture. And you said: “Mine is getting old.”
That was the beginning. From that moment… you started noticing TVs everywhere.
Scrolling at night — one appears. Watching YouTube — another one shows. Passing a shop — you slow down a bit.
Then an ad comes. Clean. Sharp. Well designed.
“Buy now.” “Best price.” “Limited stock.”
You pause. You look at it. You even imagine it in your sitting room.
Then… you scroll.
Not because you didn’t want it. But because… you weren’t ready.
And Yet… You Still Bought Eventually.
Days later. Weeks later. Maybe even a month later. You bought. But not from that ad. Not from the one that tried to rush you. Not from the one that assumed your timing.
You bought from the one that stayed with you.
The one that felt familiar. The one that somehow… understood your pace.
That Was You Inside The 67%.
Not lost. Not uninterested. Not a bad customer. Just… not ready yet.
Now Look At Your Own Business.
How many people see you every day… and do exactly what you did?
They see your post. They see your ad. They even like it. Then they scroll. And you assume: “They’re not serious.”
But what if… they’re just where you were?
Thinking. Watching. Quietly moving closer.
Now Here’s Where It Gets Uncomfortable.
If people are like this… if buying is a journey… if timing is everything…
What Exactly Are You Saying To Them Right Now?
When they are just starting to think… What do they see from you?
When they are considering… What do you give them?
When they are not ready yet… but will be… How do you stay in their mind?
Is your content built for that stage? Or only for the final moment?
Because if all you ever say is: “Buy now”, “DM me”, “Limited offer”... Then you are only speaking to people who are already at the end. And ignoring everyone else.
So Ask Yourself Honestly.
What do you show someone who is not ready to buy today… but will be ready soon?
What do you say to someone who is interested… but still thinking?
What kind of message makes them remember you even after they scroll?
What kind of presence makes them feel like: “When I’m ready… I’ll go back to this one.”
Because that moment… that exact moment… is where businesses are chosen.
Not loudly. Not instantly. But quietly. Over time.
And If You Don’t Know How To Do That Yet…
That’s the gap.
Not effort. Not consistency. But understanding how to exist inside the 67%.
Because Once You Understand That…
You don’t chase customers. You stay with them.
You don’t fight for attention. You become familiar. You don’t try to convince. You become the obvious choice.
And That Changes Everything.
